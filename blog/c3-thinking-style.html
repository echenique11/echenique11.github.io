Thinkng style and learning

2-22-2015

When I took the thinking style test on pre-Phase 0 work, I notice that my thinking style was concrete, since my result was this :

CS AS AR CR
10 2 8 10

According with Anthony Gregorc

  • Concrete Sequential
  • This learner likes:
  • order
  • logical sequence
  • following directions, predictability
  • getting facts
  • They learn best when:
  • they have a structured environment
  • they can rely on others to complete this task
  • are faced with predictable situations
  • can apply ideas in pragmatic ways
  • What's hard for them?
  • Working in groups
  • Discussions that seem to have no specific point
  • Working in an unorganized environment
  • Following incomplete or unclear directions
  • Working with unpredictable people
  • Dealing with abstract ideas
  • Demands to "use your imagination"
  • Questions with no right or wrong answers
  • Concrete Random
  • This learner likes:
  • experimenting to find answers
  • take risks
  • use their intuition
  • solving problems independently
  • They learn best when:
  • they are able to use trial-and-error approaches
  • able to compete with others
  • given the opportunity to work through the problems by themselves.
  • What's hard for them?
  • Restrictions and limitations
  • Formal reports
  • Routines
  • Re-doing anything once itâ€™s done
  • Keeping detailed records
  • Showing how they got an answer
  • Choosing only one answer
  • Having no options
I was really surprise how accurate this is, even thou I tested 10 in both CS and CR, I would say my learning style is CR or better yet I have more CR than CS. So far during phase 0, I have been struggling with pairing, basically because of the time. This past 2 weeks I was so stuck with stuff that I was coordinating and was very hard to make time for pairing. But this coming weeks I'll make it happen.
